How to Prevent Deepfakes

In a world where AI-generated audio and video are common, how can we protect ourselves?

🛡️ Prevention Tips

Use the methods below to identify and prevent deepfakes

👁️ Visual Cues to Look For

👄

Lip Sync

Does lip movement match the words spoken?

👀

Blinking

Is blinking natural and frequent?

🖼️

Image Quality

Is the facial area lower quality than the rest?

🔍

Edges

Are the edges around the face unnatural?
